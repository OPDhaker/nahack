AQI

The Air Quality Index(AQI) is a measure of air quality affecting one's health in a short time period.The higher the AQI value the higher is the level of concern.AQI values at or below 100 are generally thought of as satisfactory.

Air Pollution in India

Eight pollutants (PM10, PM2.5, NO2, CO, SO2, NH3, O3 and Pb) are the parameters taken used for deriving AQI. Of the 30 most polluted cities in the world, 21 were in India in 2019.
