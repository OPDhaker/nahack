About Us

We are Nahack. This is an organization working for the quality of air all over the world. The organization based in Madhya Pradesh(headquarter), India took the initiative seeing the horrendous situation of air pollution all over India. Since then we started the work from Delhi and now we have presence internationally. We have tied up with Team4Nature, WWF, Team Earth, Team Trees and Plant for the Planet. We plant 1 tree for each dollar we receive as donation.
